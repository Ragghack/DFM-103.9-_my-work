The Importance of Supporting Local Businesses

In these challenging economic times, I believe we need to focus more on supporting our local businesses and entrepreneurs. Too often, we prioritize imported goods over locally produced ones, which hurts our economy in the long run.

I've started consciously choosing Cameroonian products whenever possible, and I encourage others to do the same. This small change can make a significant impact on our national economy.

Education Reform Needs in Cameroon

After reading the article about economic recovery, I started thinking about how education plays a crucial role in our nation's development. We need to reform our educational system to focus more on practical skills and digital literacy.

Too many graduates lack the skills needed in today's job market. We should incorporate more technology training, entrepreneurship education, and critical thinking development into our curriculum.
