Investment Initiatives

National Development Strategy 2025

The government has outlined a comprehensive investment strategy focusing on:

  • Digital infrastructure modernization ($250 million)
  • Agricultural transformation ($180 million)
  • Energy sector expansion ($320 million)
  • Healthcare system improvement ($150 million)

These initiatives are expected to create over 50,000 jobs and boost GDP growth by 1.5% annually.
